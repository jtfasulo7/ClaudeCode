Phase 1: Brand Positioning Analysis

Understanding what LumiRecover stands for and where it should expand

What LumiRecover Stands For

LumiRecover occupies the intersection of at-home beauty tech and recovery wellness, targeting women 28-48 who want professional skincare results without clinic visits. The brand's DNA is built on:

  • Light therapy as a core technology pillar
  • Visible, dramatic results (before/after transformations)
  • Premium aesthetic at accessible prices (positioned against $300-$800 clinical devices)
  • Self-care ritual framing (not medical, not clinical -- empowering)

Natural Expansion Categories (Brand-Coherent)

  • Light therapy devices (face, neck, body, eyes) -- core brand extension
  • EMS/Microcurrent facial toning -- adjacent tech, same customer
  • Heated beauty tools (gua sha, wands) -- visual drama, ritual appeal
  • Body recovery light therapy (belts, wraps) -- extends "Recover" brand name
  • Hydration/misting devices -- visual appeal, self-care ritual
  • Eye area treatments -- high-demand sub-niche of the same customer

Categories REJECTED (Poor Brand Fit)

  • Supplements/ingestibles (different trust model)
  • Hair tools (different customer intent)
  • Basic skincare consumables (low margin, no tech angle)
  • Fitness equipment (wrong brand identity)
