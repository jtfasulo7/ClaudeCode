Our Process

How a JMartin project runs.

Five clear stages. You always know where the project is and what's next.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We meet on-site or over video. You share what you want; we share what's realistic. Free, no obligation.

  2. 02

    Design & Plans

    Architectural drawings, finishes, fixtures. We help you make decisions before they cost money to change.

  3. 03

    Permits & Pricing

    Fixed-bid, line-item proposal. No vague allowances. We pull permits and lock the schedule before swinging a hammer.

  4. 04

    Construction

    Weekly site walks, weekly written updates, single point of contact. You see progress, not surprises.

  5. 05

    Walkthrough & Warranty

    Final punch list, walkthrough, keys. One-year workmanship warranty. We answer the phone after the job is done.
