About this service

Renovations are about respecting what's there and improving what isn't. Sometimes that means cosmetic. Sometimes that means structural. Either way, it means making the right decisions about what to keep, what to remove, and what to rebuild — and being honest with you about the tradeoffs.

We pull permits, coordinate trades, and communicate every day you're living in a job site. The less surprise, the better the experience — for you and for us.

What's included

  • Demo and disposal
  • Structural changes (load-bearing, beams, posts)
  • Framing modifications
  • MEP rough-in and updates
  • Insulation and drywall
  • Trim, doors, and cabinetry
  • Tile, countertops, and stone
  • Fixtures, paint, and finish
  • Punch list and walkthrough

The honest take

Most full renovations take eight to sixteen weeks per major room or zone. We protect the rest of your house with isolation walls and dust control. If you can move out for the worst weeks, we'll tell you. If you can stay, we'll make it as livable as we can.
