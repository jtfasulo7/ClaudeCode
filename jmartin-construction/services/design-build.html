About this service

Traditional residential projects have a designer drawing dreams and a builder pricing reality, with a contract gap between them where most of the surprises live. Design-build closes that gap. Your design moves through pricing in real time, so "wouldn't this be cool" is matched against "this is what it actually costs" before you fall in love with the wrong idea.

Most clients save months on the front end and avoid the painful "redesign because the budget came back too high" loop. You sign one contract, talk to one team, and have one phone number for the entire project.

What's included

  • Architectural design
  • Structural engineering coordination
  • Interior design coordination
  • Code review and permitting
  • Real-time pricing during design
  • Single fixed-bid construction contract
  • Single point-of-contact through finish
  • One-year workmanship warranty

The honest take

Design-build works best when speed and clarity matter more than the chance to shop multiple bids on finished plans. If you already have a fully developed set of plans you love, traditional bid-and-build is fine. If you're starting from scratch, design-build will save you time, money, and heartburn.
