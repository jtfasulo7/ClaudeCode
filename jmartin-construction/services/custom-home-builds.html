About this service

A custom home is a once-in-a-lifetime project for most families. Our job is to make a complicated process feel like the simplest decision you've ever made — fixed pricing, real timelines, weekly progress, and one phone number that always works.

We handle every trade. We don't hand the project to a "project manager" you never see again after signing. The same team that walked your lot at the start is on site through finish.

What's included

  • Site survey, layout, and excavation
  • Foundation, framing, and structural
  • Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing
  • Insulation and roofing
  • Siding, windows, and exterior trim
  • Interior framing, trim, and cabinetry
  • Tile, countertops, and fixtures
  • Final walkthrough and one-year warranty

The honest take

Custom homes typically run 12–18 months from contract to keys, depending on size, site, and your decision-making pace. If you need something faster or cheaper than that range allows, a renovation or addition might serve you better. We'll tell you that on the consultation call instead of signing you up for the wrong project.
