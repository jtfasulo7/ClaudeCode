Spring start-up & fall blow-out.

Two visits a year, on schedule. In spring we charge the system slowly, check every zone, walk the property for leaks, and adjust heads. In fall — typically late October — we pull the air compressor out, evacuate every zone, and shut the system down for winter. Skip one, and you've got cracked pipes when the freeze comes.

How pricing works.

Most residential start-ups and blow-outs run $90–$180, based on zone count. Service calls start at $125 (1 hour, parts extra). A new 6-zone install typically runs $3,200–$5,500, depending on lot size and water tap location.
