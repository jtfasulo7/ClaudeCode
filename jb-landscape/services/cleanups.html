Spring and Fall Yard Cleanups in Providence, RI

The reset that makes everything else look easy. Twice a year.

Leaf removal, bed prep, edging, dethatching. The seasonal cleanup that sets up a great-looking lawn for the rest of the year.

Free Estimate (401) 230-4820

Spring cleanup.

End of winter, before the first mow. We dethatch the lawn, hand-rake out leaves and debris from beds, cut back perennials, edge beds fresh, and haul everything away. The lawn breathes; the beds look reset.

Spring includes

  • Dethatching and leaf removal
  • Cut-back of perennials and ornamental grasses
  • Bed edge re-cut
  • Stick & branch pickup
  • Pre-emergent option (priced separately)
  • Haul-away of all debris

Fall cleanup.

Late October to mid-December. We do multiple visits as leaves come down, with a final deep pass once everything has dropped. Beds get pulled clean, perennials get cut back, lawn gets one last short cut.

Fall includes

  • Repeat leaf removal (typically 2–3 visits)
  • Final deep cleanup pass
  • Perennial cut-back
  • Bed cleanup and edge
  • Last cut of the season
  • Optional gutter clean-out

How pricing works.

Most cleanups in RI run $200–$600 per visit, depending on lot size and leaf load. Large lots with mature trees can be higher. We give you the fixed number after the walk-through.
