How a mulch job runs.

We cut a fresh edge on every bed first — that crisp line is half the curb appeal. We pull visible weeds and trim back overgrowth. Then we lay 2–3 inches of fresh mulch, even, and pull it back from trunks and siding. Driveway and walks get blown clean before we leave.

What's included

  • Fresh hand-cut bed edge
  • Weed pull (visible top growth)
  • 2–3 inches of fresh mulch, evenly raked
  • Mulch pulled back from trunks & foundation
  • Blow-down on hardscape
  • Haul-away of any debris

Mulch options.

  • Hemlock — reddish-brown, premium look
  • Black-dyed — deep contrast against green
  • Brown-dyed — warm, consistent tone
  • Natural double-ground — economical, ages gray
  • Cedar — fragrant, deters some pests

How pricing works.

Most residential mulch jobs in RI run $95–$120 per cubic yard installed, including edging and weed pull. A typical front-yard refresh is 4–7 yards. Estimate is free and itemized so you see exactly what we're laying.
