What a design-and-install project looks like.

We start with a walk of the property — what works, what's not thriving, what you want from the space. We sketch a plan with you, pick plants that actually thrive in Rhode Island's climate and soil, and then our crew installs everything: prep, plant, water, mulch.

What's included

  • On-site consultation and walk-through
  • Hand-drawn or rendered plan
  • Plant selection (natives + ornamentals)
  • Grading and bed shaping
  • Sod or seed installation
  • Mulch or stone top-dressing
  • Irrigation tie-in (if applicable)
  • First-season warranty on installed plants

Plants we use most.

For RI yards we lean on what does well in zone 6b/7a — hydrangea, boxwood, inkberry, summersweet, red twig dogwood, perennials like coneflower, black-eyed susan, sedum, and ornamental grasses. We avoid anything that fights our humidity and clay.

How pricing works.

Most front-yard refreshes run $2,500–$8,000 installed. Full property installs (new build, full backyard) run $10,000–$40,000+ depending on hardscape and irrigation. Estimate is always free and itemized.
