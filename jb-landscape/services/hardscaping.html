How a hardscape project runs.

The base is everything. We excavate to depth, lay and compact 6–12 inches of crushed stone in lifts, set the screed, and only then place pavers or wallstone. Edges get restraint. Joints get polymeric sand. Done right, your patio is still flat in twenty years.

What we build

  • Paver and stone patios
  • Front walks and step systems
  • Segmental retaining walls (Techo-Bloc, Belgard, etc.)
  • Sitting walls and pillar caps
  • Fire pits — round and square
  • Outdoor kitchen pads
  • Drainage and dry wells

How pricing works.

Most paver patios in RI run $22–$35 per square foot installed, depending on paver, access, and excavation. Walls run $45–$80 per face foot. We give you a fixed price after the site walk — no T&M, no surprise upcharges.

Materials we work with.

Techo-Bloc, Belgard, Cambridge, and Unilock pavers. Natural bluestone for premium walks. Local fieldstone for accent walls. We'll bring product samples to the consultation so you can hold the actual stone in your hand before you decide.
