Overview.

We bring the truck, we load it, and we sweep up after. You point at what needs to go. That’s the whole job.

What we'll take

  • Old furniture — couches, mattresses, desks
  • Appliances — fridges, washers, dryers
  • Yard debris — branches, brush, old fencing
  • Construction leftovers — drywall, lumber, scrap
  • Garage and basement cleanouts
  • Estate cleanouts

What we can't take

  • Hazardous chemicals or paint cans
  • Tires (we can refer a local recycler)
  • Anything that requires a permit to dispose

How pricing works.

Priced by truck load — quarter-load, half-load, three-quarter-load, full load. You’ll see the truck before we start. Fixed quote, no hidden disposal fees.
