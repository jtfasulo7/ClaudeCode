Overview.

We mow at the right deck height for the season, alternate direction to avoid wear patterns, and string-trim around every bed, fence, and fixture. Hard surfaces get edged. Driveways and walkways get blown clean before we leave.

Every visit

  • Mowing at the right deck height for the season
  • String-trimming around beds, fences, posts
  • Hard-edge along walks, drives, and curbs
  • Blow-down on all hardscape surfaces
  • Bag, mulch, or side-discharge — your call
  • Quick walk before we leave

Seasonal extras (priced separately)

  • Spring & fall cleanups
  • Dethatching and aeration
  • Overseeding and lime
  • Bed mulching and weed pull
  • Shrub trimming and pruning

What you can expect.

You don't need to be home. We text the morning of, mow within our window, and text again when we're done — usually with a photo. Pay weekly or get a monthly invoice. Cancel any time.
