FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Don't see yours? Call us at (401) 230-4820.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Every estimate is free, with no obligation. We come out, walk the property, and give you a real number — usually within the same week. If the job is small enough to quote over the phone, we'll do that too.

What towns in Rhode Island do you service?

Our regular service area is Greater Providence — Providence, Cranston, Warwick, East Providence, Pawtucket, North Providence, Johnston, Smithfield, and Lincoln. If you're elsewhere in Rhode Island, call us — we probably still cover it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, fully licensed and insured. We carry general liability and workers' compensation. Happy to provide a certificate of insurance for any project — just ask.

How often do you mow during the season?

Most residential lawns get weekly service from late April through October. In the dry stretches of mid-summer we'll drop to every other week to protect the turf — you only pay for the visits we make.

Do you do one-time jobs, or only weekly contracts?

Both. We're happy to do one-time mulching, cleanups, patios, or hardscape projects. Weekly customers get scheduling priority but everyone gets the same crew and the same quality.
