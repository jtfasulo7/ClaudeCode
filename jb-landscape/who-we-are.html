Who We Are

Meet the team behind J.B. Landscape.

Built by family. Grown across three states.

I spent years working as a landscaper for other companies before my son and I started J.B. Landscape. We saw the same things every season — corners cut, jobs left half-finished, customers who deserved better than what they were getting. So we decided to do this differently. We wanted to build a landscaping company that actually showed up, did the work right, and treated people the way we'd want to be treated ourselves.

That's how J.B. Landscape started, and that's still how we run it today.

We're based in Rhode Island and we serve the entire state — from Providence and Warwick down to Newport, Westerly, and the South County beach towns. We also work across the border in eastern Connecticut (New London County and Windham County) and southeastern Massachusetts (Bristol County, Plymouth County, and the Cape area). If you're in our part of New England and you need landscape work done right, we're probably already working in your neighborhood.

What we do

We handle the full range of residential and light commercial landscaping. That includes:

  • Weekly and bi-weekly lawn care and maintenance
  • Landscape design and installation
  • Mulching, edging, and bed maintenance
  • Hardscaping and outdoor features
  • Spring and fall property cleanups
  • Full property transformations and yard makeovers

Whether you need someone reliable to keep your lawn looking sharp through the season, or you're ready to completely redesign your outdoor space, we approach every job the same way. We show up when we say we will. We do the work right the first time. We leave the property looking better than when we got there.

Why we do it this way

Running J.B. Landscape as a family business has shaped how we operate from day one. A few things we hold to:

Craftsmanship matters.

A lawn or a landscape job done well stays done well. The shortcuts always show up later — patchy grass, dying plants, beds falling apart by midsummer. I'd rather take the time to do it right the first time than come back to fix something I should have done properly to begin with.

We show up.

The number one complaint homeowners have about landscapers is the no-shows and the ghosting. If we say we'll be at your property on Tuesday, we're at your property on Tuesday. That's it. It's not complicated, but it's apparently rare in this industry.

Every customer is treated like family.

This isn't a slogan for me — it's how my son and I were raised in the business. When you work for someone for years, they stop being a customer and start being a relationship. The properties we maintain weekly, the homeowners we've redesigned full landscapes for — those are the people we're building this company for.

We actually care about the work.

Landscaping isn't just a job for me. There's something about transforming an overgrown property into a clean, healthy outdoor space that doesn't get old. My son grew up watching this work get done, and now he's running crews alongside me. That continuity matters.

Our promise to you

If we show up at your property, we leave it looking its absolute best. That applies whether we're there for a 30-minute mowing visit or a multi-week landscape installation. Quality work and satisfied customers are the only outcomes I'm working toward.

We're proud to be growing J.B. Landscape as a family-run business serving Rhode Island and the surrounding region. We're excited to keep building lasting relationships with homeowners and property owners across our area, one yard at a time.

If you're looking for a landscaper who shows up, does the work right, and treats your property like it matters — give us a call. We'd be glad to come take a look.
