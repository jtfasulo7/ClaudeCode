Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

J.B. Landscape (“we”, “us”) operates the website at jblandscape.com. This page explains what information we collect when you visit or submit a form on the site, and how we use that information.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and the message you provide. We also automatically log the IP address, browser user-agent, and the page URL the form was submitted from, for the purpose of preventing abuse.

How we use it

The contact-form information is used only to respond to your inquiry, provide an estimate, and follow up on services. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing.

SMS consent

If you check the box agreeing to be contacted by text message, we may send you SMS messages about your estimate and scheduled service. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Service providers

We use Vercel to host the website and GoHighLevel to process lead submissions. Both providers have their own privacy policies.

Cookies

We do not set marketing cookies. The site does not currently use analytics tracking.
