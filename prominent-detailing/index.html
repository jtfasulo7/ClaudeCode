FAQ

Asked & answered.

How long does ceramic coating actually last?
The 5-year tier we install is rated for 5 years with proper maintenance washes (every 4–6 weeks). The 9-year flagship has a transferable warranty backed by the manufacturer. We recommend an annual maintenance wash to keep the warranty active — it's included with the package.
Do you come to me, or do I come to you?
Both. Express and Premium packages can be done at your home or office (we bring water and power). Showroom packages with paint correction or ceramic coating are done in our temperature-controlled shop because dust control and curing matter.
What if it rains the day of my appointment?
For mobile jobs we'll reach out the morning of with a recommendation. We never start a paint correction in marginal conditions. Rescheduling is free up to 24 hours before your slot.
Will paint correction damage my clear coat?
No. We measure paint depth before and after every stage and never remove more than necessary. You'll get the readings in your before/after report so you know exactly what was done.
How do I maintain the finish between visits?
Hand wash, never automatic. Two-bucket method with a pH-neutral soap. Avoid touch tunnels and brush washes. We send a maintenance kit + walkthrough video with every Showroom package.
Do you guarantee your work?
Yes. 7-day re-correction window — if anything's not right, we make it right at no charge. Ceramic coatings carry the manufacturer's warranty, registered in your name.
