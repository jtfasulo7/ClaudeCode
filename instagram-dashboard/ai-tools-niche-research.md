## 4. HOOK FORMULAS THAT WORK

### The Science: You Have 1.7-3 Seconds

- 71% of viewers decide within the first 3 seconds whether to keep watching
- Generic openings ("Hey guys, today I want to talk about...") lose 73% of viewers before second 4
- Script your hook word-for-word; this is too critical to improvise
- 85% of users watch on mute; your hook must work visually AND with text

### 15 Proven Hook Formulas for AI Tools Content

**CURIOSITY HOOKS:**
1. "No one talks about this, but..." - *"No one talks about this ChatGPT feature, but it replaces $500/month in tools."*
2. "Most people don't know this, but..." - *"Most people don't know this, but Claude can do your entire market research in 10 minutes."*
3. "Here's what they don't tell you about..." - *"Here's what they don't tell you about using AI for content."*
4. "Only 1% of people know this..." - *"Only 1% of ChatGPT users know this prompt trick."* (shareability increases 80%)

**PAIN POINT HOOKS:**
5. "Stop doing [X]. Here's why." - *"Stop paying for Canva Pro. Here's why."*
6. "You're doing [X] wrong. Here's what actually works." - *"You're using ChatGPT wrong. Here's what actually works."*
7. "3 mistakes you're making with [X]" - *"3 mistakes you're making with AI that are wasting hours."* (outperform generic advice 2:1)
8. "The mistake that cost me [X]" - *"The mistake that cost me 20,000 followers."*

**TRANSFORMATION HOOKS:**
9. "I went from [X] to [Y] in [timeframe]" - *"I went from 0 to $5K/month using only free AI tools."* (217% increase in lead acquisition)
10. "This changed the way I [do something] forever" - *"This changed the way I create content forever."*
11. "Watch me [do something] in [short time]" - *"Watch me build an entire website in 60 seconds with AI."*

**AUTHORITY HOOKS:**
12. "My clients pay me to tell them this" - *"My clients pay me $200/hr to tell them this AI workflow."*
13. "If I had to start from zero, here's exactly what I'd do" - *"If I had to start an AI business from zero today, here's what I'd do."*
14. "Before you [action], watch this" - *"Before you pay for another AI tool, watch this."*

**DIRECT CALL-OUT:**
15. "If you're a [target audience], stop scrolling!" - *"If you're a freelancer not using AI yet, stop scrolling!"* (91.7% more attention)

### High-Performance Hook Patterns Specific to AI Niche
- **Negative framing** consistently outperforms positive ("Stop doing X" > "Start doing X")
- **Specific numbers** in hooks increase click-through ("Save 10 hours" > "Save time")
- **Tool name-drops** create instant relevance ("This ChatGPT trick..." > "This AI trick...")
- **Money hooks** drive highest initial engagement ("$X" in hook)
- **Time-saving hooks** drive highest saves ("in 60 seconds," "in 5 minutes")

- Educational content generates **2.3x more saves** than entertainment

- Saves drive **3-5x better reach** because the algorithm heavily weights them

**Recommended Ratio:**
- **70% Educational/Value** (tutorials, tips, how-tos, tool demos)
- **20% Entertaining/Relatable** (AI fails, humor, trending formats with AI twist)

- 1-2 subtle product showcase videos (Blotato features shown IN a workflow, not pitched)

- All roads lead to the newsletter (the "true conversion engine")
- Email = owned audience, algorithm-proof
- Sabrina's newsletter: 118K subscribers with **40% open rate**
- Place CTA in PS at the end, after delivering free value

### Key BOFU Principle
Instagram warms the audience and then hands off to retargeting, email, or on-site experiences. Don't try to close cold traffic directly on Instagram. ## 7. SCRIPT STRUCTURES THAT WORK

### The Core Viral Script Formula

```
HOOK (0-3 seconds) → PROBLEM (3-15 seconds) → SOLUTION (15-45 seconds) → CTA (final 5 seconds)
```

Target: **70%+ completion rate** for algorithmic boost.

### Detailed Breakdown

**HOOK (0-3 sec) - Stop the Scroll**
- Script word-for-word (never improvise)
- Must work visually AND with text (85% watch on mute)
- Pattern interrupt: say or show something unexpected
- Text overlay on screen matches spoken hook exactly

**PROBLEM (3-15 sec) - Create Tension**
- Identify the pain point your viewer feels
- Make it specific and relatable
- "Most people spend 3 hours doing X manually..."
- "If you're still using [old method], you're wasting time..."

**SOLUTION (15-45 sec) - Deliver the Payoff**
- Show, don't just tell
- Screen recordings of the actual AI tool in action
- Scene changes every 2-4 seconds to maintain attention
- Keep it to 3 strong benefits max (tight 150 words > 200 words with filler)
- Information density matters more than length

**CTA (Final 5 sec) - Single Clear Action**
- Choose ONE action: follow, comment a keyword, save, share, or click link in bio
- One CTA converts better than multiple
- Instagram allows slightly longer CTAs (2-3 sentences) vs. TikTok

### Example Script: AI Tool Tutorial Reel (35 seconds)

```
[HOOK - 3 sec]
"Stop paying for [expensive tool]. This free AI does the same thing."

[PROBLEM - 7 sec]
"Most people are spending $50 a month on [tool] when ChatGPT
can do this for free. Here's the exact prompt I use."

[SOLUTION - 20 sec]
[Screen recording of ChatGPT]
"Step 1: Open ChatGPT and paste this prompt..."
"Step 2: Upload your [data/image/document]..."
"Step 3: Watch it generate [result] in seconds."
"I just saved you $600 a year."

[CTA - 5 sec]
"Save this for later, and follow for more free AI tools
that replace expensive software."
```

### Platform-Specific Adjustments
- **Instagram Reels:** 3-second hooks, polished aesthetics, under 30 seconds for new audiences, 30-45 for established
- **TikTok:** 2-second hooks, 20% higher energy, 15-30 seconds total
- **YouTube Shorts:** 3-5 second hooks, 60-second maximum, searchable titles

Instagram CEO Adam Mosseri has publicly confirmed that **hashtags no longer directly boost reach**. The algorithm now prioritizes:
1. Content quality

## 10. COMMON MISTAKES

### Mistake #1: Over-Relying on AI-Generated Content
Pure AI-generated content without human touch underperforms. Instagram actively detects duplicate sounds, recycled formats, and heavily templated visuals. **Use AI to enhance, not replace, your voice.**

### Mistake #2: No Niche Clarity
Instagram's algorithm became significantly more sophisticated in December 2025 at penalizing accounts that lack clear positioning. If content jumps between unrelated topics, you get removed from interest lists. **Commit to "AI tools" as your lane.**

### Mistake #3: Weak Hooks / Slow Intros
Generic openings like "Hey guys" lose 73% of viewers. Highly produced videos with long intros underperform compared to raw, quick ones with immediate hooks. **A competitor's iPhone-shot video with an immediate hook outperformed highly edited content 5-10x in views.**

### Mistake #4: Optimizing for Likes Instead of Shares/Saves
Likes are now the **weakest** engagement signal. A video watched to completion by 200 people outperforms a video liked by 2,000 but abandoned after 3 seconds. **Optimize for watch time, shares, and saves.**

### Mistake #5: Straight Copycat Content
Instagram deprioritizes generic trend-hopping without a unique twist. If you hop on a trend, you MUST add your niche perspective. **A straight copycat is algorithmically invisible.**

### Mistake #6: Over-Posting with Low Engagement
Excessive posting with low engagement damages distribution. The algorithm interprets repeated underperforming posts as audience disinterest. **Better to post 3 great pieces than 7 mediocre ones.**

### Mistake #7: Reposting Too Much
Accounts posting 10+ reposts within 30 days get excluded from recommendations entirely. Original content receives 40-60% more distribution. **Create original content.**

### Mistake #8: Multiple CTAs
Asking viewers to "like, comment, follow, share, and click link in bio" dilutes action. **Pick ONE CTA per piece of content.**

### Mistake #9: Not Funneling to Owned Channels
Relying entirely on Instagram without building email/newsletter means you don't own your audience. One algorithm change can wipe out your reach. **Always funnel to email.**

### Mistake #10: Ignoring Captions/SEO
With hashtags deprioritized, your caption text IS your discoverability. Creators who write keyword-rich, searchable captions get recommended more. **Write captions like you're writing for search.**
