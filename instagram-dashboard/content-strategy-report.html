Proven Hook Formulas for AI Content

You have 1.7 seconds. These are the hook structures that stop the scroll in the AI niche.

#FormulaExampleWhy It Works
1 Negative Command "Stop using ChatGPT like this." Creates anxiety + curiosity. People need to know what they're doing wrong.
2 Shocking Claim "This AI tool just made Photoshop obsolete." Bold claims create debate. Debate = comments = distribution.
3 Specific Number "I saved 10 hours this week with one AI tool." Specificity = credibility. Vague claims get scrolled past.
4 Identity Call-Out "If you're a freelancer, stop scrolling." Targets a specific person. They feel seen and stop.
5 Secret/Hidden "No one talks about this Claude feature." Exclusivity triggers. People want info others don't have.
6 Before/After "This took 3 hours to edit. Now it takes 30 seconds." Visual contrast is irresistible. Works exceptionally for AI tools.
7 Relatable Pain "POV: Your boss asks you to learn AI by Monday." Humor + shared experience = shares to coworkers/friends.
8 Future Prediction "In 6 months, people who know this will be ahead." Fear of missing out. Urgency without being salesy.
